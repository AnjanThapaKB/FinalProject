About

I’m Anjan Thapa.

Originally from the beautiful land of Nepal and now embracing the enchanting landscapes and vibrant culture of Finland. My journey has been one of cultural exploration and academic pursuit, with each step shaping my experiences and perspectives. Having spent the formative years of my life in Nepal, I carry with me the rich heritage, traditions, and warmth of Nepalese culture. It’s a part of me that intertwines seamlessly with my current life in Finland, adding depth and diversity to my everyday experiences. My decision to move to Finland in January 2023 marked a significant chapter in my life. It’s here that I’ve delved into the world of Information Technology through my Bachelor’s studies in Engineering. The fusion of theoretical knowledge and practical skills has not only fueled my academic growth but also opened doors to new opportunities and insights into the tech industry.

As a student living in Finland, I've come to cherish the unique blend of nature, culture, and education that defines this Nordic gem. Through my blogsite, I invite you to embark on a journey of discovery alongside me. Picture yourself stepping into a Finnish forest, surrounded by towering pines and the crisp scent of pine needles underfoot. That sense of calm and wonder mirrors the experience of delving into my blogsite. Each post is a trail to explore, offering insights into Finnish traditions, academic adventures, and the intersections of global cultures. Just as Finland embraces both modernity and tradition, my blogsite embraces a diverse range of topics. From academic tips and insights gained from my studies to reflections on Finnish lifestyle and culture, there's something here for every curious mind. Much like navigating the interconnected waterways of Finland’s archipelago, you'll find yourself seamlessly navigating between posts that inspire, inform, and entertain. One of the joys of living in Finland is its strong sense of community, and I strive to replicate that spirit on my blogsite. Let’s embark on this journey together!

Interests and Hobbies

As a travel blogger, my interests and hobbies revolve around exploring new cultures, discovering hidden gems, and capturing moments through photography. I'm passionate about trying local cuisines, immersing myself in diverse landscapes, and sharing authentic travel experiences with my audience. Whether it's hiking in remote mountains, wandering through bustling markets, or uncovering historical landmarks, I thrive on the thrill of adventure and the joy of storytelling through my travel journeys.
